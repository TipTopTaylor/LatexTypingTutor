Loading Strategy
================
1. legacy.js loads first (regular script)
   ↓
2. app.js loads second (ES6 module)
   ↓
3. app.js imports all modules
   ↓
4. Modules exposed on window.latexModules
   ↓
5. Legacy code can use modular functions
   ↓
6. Application initializes
